Horseback Riding Day at El Kabayo

Clark, Pampanga

Duration: 2 hours

Difficulty: Low

Participants: 6 max

Description

Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Clark, Pampanga, at El Kabayo's horseback riding experience. Explore the scenic landscapes, charming villages, and rustic farms and ranches that make this area unique. El Kabayo is nestled in the picturesque surroundings, offering a delightful adventure for nature enthusiasts.

Discover the allure of Clark's hills, known for their rich history of mineral extraction. A knowledgeable local guide will lead you to the most captivating spots, providing insights into the authentic spirit of the region's inhabitants.

This horseback riding excursion is designed for everyone, from beginners to experienced riders, seeking tranquility, nature, and a connection with these extraordinary animals. Whether you're a seasoned rider or trying it for the first time, El Kabayo welcomes you to an unforgettable experience.

Who is it for?

This activity is suitable for riders of all levels, and participants must be at least 14 years old and weigh no more than 85 kg.

Recommended Attire

Long and comfortable pants and closed-toe shoes.

Included Equipment

- Helmet

- Gloves

- Boots

- Pants
