Wakeboarding

Deca Clark, Pampanga

Duration: 1 hour

Difficulty: Medium

Participants: 12 max

Description

Embark on an exhilarating wakeboarding adventure at Deca Clark Watersports. Glide through the refreshing waters surrounded by picturesque landscapes. Our expert instructors will guide you through the thrilling experience, ensuring a memorable time in the heart of Deca Clark.

This wakeboarding activity is perfect for water enthusiasts, whether you're a seasoned rider or a first-timer. Open to all levels, join us for an unforgettable aquatic adventure that combines excitement and the beauty of Deca Clark.

Who is it for?

This wakeboarding experience is designed for participants of all levels, providing a thrilling time for everyone. Whether you're an experienced wakeboarder or a beginner looking for excitement, our instructors will tailor the experience to your comfort and skill level. Dive into the waters and join us for a memorable wakeboarding session at Deca Clark.

Recommended Attire

Dress comfortably for the water, ensuring a safe and enjoyable wakeboarding adventure.

Included Equipment

- Wakeboard

- Life Jacket

- Tow Handle

- Wetsuit
